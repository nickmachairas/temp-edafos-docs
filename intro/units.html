Input Units¶

Input units for geotechnical and foundation attributes.

  S.I. English
Depth to Water Table meters feet
Soil Layer Height meters feet
Total Unit Weight kN/m3 lbf/ft3 [1]
Soil Stresses kN/m2 kip/ft2
Pile Diameter centimeters inches
Pipe Pile Wall Thickness centimeters inches
Pile Cross Section (Area) cm2 in2
H-Pile d, bf, tw, tf centimeters inches
Pile Length meters feet
Pile No-Friction Depth meters feet

[1]Internally, total unit weight in the English system is converted to kip/ft3.
